About Shaheed park





Al-Shaheed park is one of the best outdoor places that you can visit in Kuwait.
It is perfect to walk and do your sport routine . Not only that but also there are many events in the park (you can read the details in events section).
In addition, there are some resturants and coffee shops so you can set and watch the pretty green view.
Not just for sport and eat, there is also a meuseum that you can visit.
Don`t forget that there is a dancing fountain every 30 minutes.










































































Events in Shaheed park

From time to time Shaheed park organaize some outdoor events in the park.

Sometimes they organaize events or workshops like outdoor sport and yoga classes.

And Sometimes shaheed park shows a movie in the park with bean chairs, it is super beautiful specially in the winter. you can book for events in their website.
