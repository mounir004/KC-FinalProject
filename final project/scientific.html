About Scientific center





Scientific Center is one of the old and famous places in Kuwait.

It is the perfect place for kids. It has an aquarium that kids can enjoy watching the fishes.

Also, there is imax ceinma so the can watch 3D scientific films.


































































Scientific Center is located on the seafront, So you also can walk along the sea.

In addition, their are some places if you like fishing.
